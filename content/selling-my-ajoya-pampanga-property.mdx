## <span id="why-ajoya-pampanga">[Why Ajoya Pampanga](#why-ajoya-pampanga)</span>

This isn't just a house—it's a whole community, built by AboitizLand, it's a new a development with all the perks and couple of vecinos already settling in:

- **Sweet Amenities:** Clubhouse, parks, playgrounds, basketball court, a pool, and 24/7 security so you can relax.
- **Proximity:** 36 minutes from Clark International Airport—perfect for a local work or travel. Plus, you've got:
  - **Malls:** SM City Pampanga (in San Fernando, about 15–20 km away) and Robinsons Starmills (also in San Fernando) are accessible within 20–30 minutes, offering shopping, dining, and entertainment. Perfect for family weekends!
  - **Schools:** Places like Chevalier School (in Angeles City, ~10–15 km) and local spots like Mexico Central School are a quick 10–20-minute drive, making school runs a breeze for families with kids.
  - **Hospitals:** Mexico Community Hospital and other facilities in San Fernando or Angeles City are within 15–25 minutes, so healthcare's always close by.
- **Growing Area:** Pampanga's real estate is growing with projects like the Manila-Clark Railway (set to make commuting even easier by 2026–2027). Mexico, Pampanga is set to benefit on this but chill enough for that quite suburban family life.
