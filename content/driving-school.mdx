## <span id="classroom">[Classroom](#classroom)</span>

I arrived at their training center in Mandaluyong. Everyone working there appeared quite professional. There were about 30 of us, and we started right on time.

A lot of discussions, divided into:

- Knowing the mechanisms of your car
- Defensive driving and traffic rules
- Rights as a licensed driver

The class began with 1–2 hours on basic vehicle operation, then focused on defensive driving, traffic rules, car requirements (garage, insurance), and implementing authorities. It concluded with driver rights.

## <span id="exam">[Exam](#exam)</span>

Morning: 60-point quiz on day one content, then scenario review. The 120-point final (85% passing) came about two hours before the end, while others took practical bike tests outside. I was one of the last to finish. Results would be ready at my branch in 2–3 days.

## <span id="results">[Results:](#results)</span>

I followed up with my branch in about a week, and (drum roll 🥁)... I passed with a mark of 107/120.
