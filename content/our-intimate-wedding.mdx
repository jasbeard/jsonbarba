## <span id="venue">[Venue](#venue)</span>

Last October, we exchanged vows at Lola Cafe, QC's Glass House—a venue with transparent walls and natural light—for our intimate gathering of 35 friends and family. This was the biggest budget item.

<br />

## <span id="planning">[Planning](#planning)</span>

The journey to our wedding day was as memorable as the event itself, thanks to our meticulous planning using **_Notion_**. Here's how we broke it down:

- **Suppliers**: My wife led vendor selection—florist, coordinators, and sound.
- **Cost Management**: I tracked every expense in Notion on a shared page.
- **Venue**: The Glass House was reasonably priced, plus food.
- **Catering**: Provided by the venue, we just had to choose from the menu.
- **Decorations**: Flowers, lighting, and table theme and arrangement.
- **Photography and Videography**: We hired people specializing in intimate weddings.
- **Miscellaneous**: Everything from invitations to thank you gifts.

This system not only helped us manage our finances but also kept us aligned on every aspect of our planning.

<br />

## <span id="the-day">[The Day](#the-day)</span>

Clear skies, then a brief cozy drizzle on the glass roof, then sun again. Here's how the rest unfolded:

- **Ceremony**: Simple yet elegant, with vows exchanged under a floral arch, the natural light and greenery outside setting a serene scene.
- **Reception**: The transition to the reception was seamless as it was in the same place, which allowed for ease, intimate conversations and laughter.
- **Moments Captured**: They captured both traditional shots and candid moments, serving as a beautiful reminder of the day.

## <span id="reflections">[Reflections](#reflections)</span>

Our wedding showed that smaller, personal ceremonies can be just as meaningful. Seeing our choices resonate with guests has been heartening. For those considering a similar path: it's about connection, not crowd size.

<br />

## <span id="guest-feedback">[Guest Feedback](#guest-feedback)</span>

- Many expressed a newfound appreciation for smaller weddings, highlighting the warmth and closeness of the event.
- Some questions were: "How much did all this cost?" While we didn't share exact numbers, we gave them a range and explained that by halving the guest list, we more than halved our expenses, making a luxurious experience affordable.
- Some guests even started brainstorming their own small wedding ideas, asking for tips on vendors and planning tools like Notion, which helped us so much.
