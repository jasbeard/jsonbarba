## <span id="steak-and-frice-bgc">[Steak & Frice, BGC](#steak--frice-bgc)</span>

A week before our anniversary, we made a reservation at Steak & Frice.
When prompted about the occasion, I casually selected _Anniversary_ from the list, not expecting much. Little did we know, this choice would turn the day even better.

## <span id="the-meal">[The Meal](#the-meal)</span>

- **Parmesan soup:** Rich and creamy texture setting the stage for slow evening.
- **Ratatouille:** Just like in the movie, as their menu said.
- **Ribeye:** Cooked to medium-rare, with rice and fries. As the name of the place suggest.

![Ribeye steak](https://yzft9j8wzzlydetx.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/blog/2025-anniversary/IMG_4352%20Large-LRJp7KdLKhy2M7h0xnvruwCbk0eO7O.jpeg "Ribeye steak")

## <span id="the-unexpected-surprise">[The Unexpected Surprise](#the-unexpected-surprise)</span>

The staff surprised us with an Instax camera for a mini photoshoot at our table, then brought a small, beautifully decorated anniversary cake. These thoughtful gestures, sparked by our reservation note, made the evening unforgettable.

![The House's Gift](https://yzft9j8wzzlydetx.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/blog/2025-anniversary/IMG_4372%20Large-GSZeqBvnL2oq8vxa9t8y6zgBI7tEGw.jpeg "The House's Gift")

## <span id="after-dinner">[After Dinner](#after-dinner)</span>

Following our meal, we walked around BGC to wind down, then treated ourselves to a massage at Big Apple Spa & Massage, enhancing the relaxation and joy of the day.

## <span id="conclusion">[Conclusion](#conclusion)</span>

Steak & Frice BGC — warm lights, instax and good food.
