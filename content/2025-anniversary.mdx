## <span id="romantic-dinner-at-steak-and-frice-bgc">[Romantic Dinner at Steak & Frice, BGC](#romantic-dinner-at-steak--frice-bgc)</span>

A week before our anniversary, we decided to make a reservation at Steak & Frice through a <ExternalLink href="https://dub.sh/jb/steakandfrice">reservation website</ExternalLink>.
When prompted about the occasion, I casually selected _Anniversary_ from the list, not expecting much from it. Little did we know, this choice would turn our meal into something truly special.

## <span id="the-arrival">[The Arrival](#the-arrival)</span>

With the wife pink rose in hand, we arrived at the restaurant, immediately welcomed by the warm, modern ambiance of Steak & Frice. The staff's friendly greeting set a lovely tone for the evening.

## <span id="the-meal">[The Meal](#the-meal)</span>

- **Parmesan soup:** We began with a comforting soup, its rich and creamy texture setting the stage for an evening of indulgence.
- **Ratatouille:** Yea, just like in the movie as their menu said. I'm a first-timer, and it seems I have a standard taste to guide me moving forward. A tasty and visual feast to our plates. Compliments to the chef! (I hope you got the reference, random reader 😁)
- **Ribeye steak:** Cooked to a perfect medium-rare, it had a delightful crisp exterior and a tender, juicy interior—a combination I truly enjoy.
  Paired with the house’s signature steak rice and fries, it showcased the exceptional quality organization in the kitchen and the masterful touch of the Chef.
- **Vanilla & Strawberry Millefeuille:** A delicate dessert where the crisp layers of pastry were beautifully contrasted with the sweet, fresh strawberries and smooth vanilla cream. It was during this course that we were treated to an unexpected surprise.

## <span id="the-unexpected-surprise">[The Unexpected Surprise](#the-unexpected-surprise)</span>

As we were savoring our dinner, something magical happened. The staff brought out an Instax camera, surprising us with a mini photoshoot right at our table. The joy of capturing our moments together, right there in the restaurant, was delightful.
But the surprises didn't stop there. Out came a small, beautifully decorated cake, courtesy of the house, celebrating our anniversary. This thoughtful gesture, sparked by that simple reservation detail, transformed our dinner into an unforgettable experience.

![The House's Gift](https://yzft9j8wzzlydetx.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/blog/2025-anniversary/IMG_4372%20Large-GSZeqBvnL2oq8vxa9t8y6zgBI7tEGw.jpeg "The House's Gift")

## <span id="the-experience-and-service">[The Experience and Service](#the-experience-and-service)</span>

Steak & Frice managed to blend fine dining with heartfelt celebration. The personal touch of the photoshoot and the surprise cake added layers of joy to our evening, making it about more than just the food.
The service was nothing short of excellent. The servers were attentive, knowledgeable, and genuinely seemed to enjoy making our evening special. This was evident when we reached the dessert portion of our meal.

## <span id="after-dinner">[After Dinner](#after-dinner)</span>

Following our meal, we walked around BGC to wind down our perfect evening, we treated ourselves to a massage at Big Apple Spa & Massage, enhancing the relaxation and joy of the day.

## <span id="conclusion">[Conclusion](#conclusion)</span>

Steak & Frice in BGC Taguig is not just a place for a meal but a venue where memories are made. The combination of exceptional food, outstanding service, and those unexpected, personal touches made our anniversary dinner one for the books.

If you're planning a special night out, do consider Steak & Frice - and don't hesitate to share your occasion; you might just find yourself pleasantly surprised by their hospitality. Here's to more anniversaries, hopefully with more surprises from this wonderful restaurant.
